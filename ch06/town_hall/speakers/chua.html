San Joaquin Valley Town Hall

Amy Chua

Amy Chua is a Yale Law professor and the best-selling author of Battle Hymn of the Tiger Mother. Her work centers on the intersection of culture, law, and success. She has been a vocal commentator on issues of parenting, education, and global competitiveness, sparking both acclaim and debate across the nation.

Chua's talk will explore the cultural forces that shape family values, academic success, and leadership, while encouraging an open dialogue about achievement and personal growth.
