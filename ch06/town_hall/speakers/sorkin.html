Andrew Ross Sorkin

Andrew Ross Sorkin is a financial columnist for The New York Times and a co-anchor of CNBC's Squawk Box. As the author of the best-selling book Too Big to Fail, Sorkin is renowned for his insight into Wall Street, the global financial system, and corporate deal-making at the highest levels.

Join us for a compelling discussion about the ever-evolving financial landscape, the moral and ethical challenges facing business leaders, and the lessons we can learn from past crises.
