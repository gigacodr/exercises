San Joaquin Valley Town Hall

David Brancaccio

David Brancaccio is an award-winning American radio and television journalist, best known for his work on public radio's Marketplace and PBS's NOW. With a focus on in-depth financial and social reporting, Brancaccio has a gift for explaining the complexities of global markets in an accessible way.

In this special talk, Brancaccio will share his insights on current economic issues, changes in media, and the global forces shaping our day-to-day lives.
